Chicken Alfredo

Creamy Chicken Alfredo with tender chicken, fettuccine, and a rich parmesan cream sauce. Quick and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt, pepper, parsley to garnish

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Season and cook chicken in a skillet until done; set aside.
  3. Melt butter in the same skillet, add garlic, then pour in heavy cream and simmer.
  4. Stir in Parmesan until sauce thickens. Add pasta and chicken; toss. Use reserved pasta water to loosen sauce if needed.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve.
