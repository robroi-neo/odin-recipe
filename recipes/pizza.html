Pizza

Classic homemade pizza with a crisp crust, rich tomato sauce, and melted cheese. Customize toppings as you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) and if available, place a pizza stone inside.
  2. Stretch dough to desired size. Brush edges with olive oil.
  3. Spread tomato sauce, sprinkle mozzarella, add toppings.
  4. Bake 8–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Let cool slightly, slice, and serve.
