Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese, baked to perfection. It's hearty, comforting, and perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce, simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and remaining cheeses on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
